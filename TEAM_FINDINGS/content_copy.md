## Tone & Style Guide for Implementation

### Voice Characteristics

1. **Personal & Warm**: Use "I" and "you" frequently. This is Osama speaking directly to potential guests.

2. **Knowledgeable but Not Arrogant**: Share expertise through stories and specific details, not boasting.

3. **Reassuring**: Safety and comfort should be woven throughout, especially for content aimed at beginners.

4. **Enthusiastic**: Genuine excitement for diving should come through without excessive exclamation marks.

5. **Locally Authentic**: References to Bedouin culture, Sinai landscape, and Egyptian hospitality add uniqueness.

### Words to Use
- Discover, explore, experience
- Safe, comfortable, confident
- Crystal-clear, pristine, vibrant
- Local, authentic, personal
- Journey, adventure, memory

### Words to Avoid
- Extreme, crazy, insane (too aggressive)
- Cheap, bargain (devalues experience)
- Tourist (use "visitor" or "diver")
- Amazing, awesome (overused, meaningless)

### Formatting Guidelines
- Short paragraphs (max 3 sentences)
- Use bullet points for scannable information
- Bold key benefits and numbers
- Break up text with subheadings
